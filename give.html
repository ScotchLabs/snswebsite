Give to Scotch'n'Soda Theatre

Support the magic of theatre and friendship

We appreciate any and all donations from students, alumni, and fans of Scotch'n'Soda Theatre. Donations can be made through Carnegie Mellon Giving to the organization to help fund our productions and activities, to our alumni organization to support alumni engagement, or to our endowment.

Subtroupes

The university will not recognize designations of specific subtroupes of Scotch'n'Soda Theatre. If you would like to give directly to a subtroupe, please make a donation towards Scotch'n'Soda Theatre and email snstheatre.md@gmail.com with information about your donation and where you would like it to go.
