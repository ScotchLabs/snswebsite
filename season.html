Shot'o'Scotch

We have an exciting new project coming soon called Shot'o'Scotch! The goal is to have smaller productions than our typical mainstage shows. This spring, we will be having two performances!

First is a reading of Twelve Angry Jurors, a play that contemplates the huge responsibility of 12 ordinary people who must decide the guilt or innocence of a teenager accused of murder. The performance will be on March 20, 2026, at 7 PM in Tepper Simmons Auditorium.

Then, the next week we will be doing a low-tech singthrough of Falsettos, a musical centered around a man who leaves his dysfunctional Jewish family for another man. The performace will be on March 27, 2026, at 7 PM in CUC McConomy.
